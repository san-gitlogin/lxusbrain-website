1. Executive Summary

TermiVoxed is designed with security as a foundational principle. We understand that enterprises trust us with their content and data, and we take this responsibility seriously. This whitepaper provides a comprehensive overview of our security practices, technical controls, and compliance posture.

Key Security Highlights

  • 100% Local Processing Option: Videos can be processed entirely on your infrastructure without leaving your network
  • End-to-End Encryption: AES-256 encryption for data at rest, TLS 1.2+ for data in transit
  • GDPR & DPDP Compliant: Full compliance with international and Indian data protection regulations
  • No Data Retention: Video content is processed and immediately deleted unless explicitly saved by the user
  • Device Binding: Secure device-based licensing with up to 50 devices per enterprise account

2. Security Architecture

2.1 Architecture Overview

TermiVoxed employs a multi-layered security architecture that protects data at every stage of processing. Our architecture is designed around the principle of defense in depth.

4. Authentication & Access Control

4.1 Authentication Methods

Standard Authentication

Email/password with secure hashing (bcrypt with cost factor 12). Password requirements: minimum 8 characters, complexity rules enforced.

OAuth 2.0

Sign in with Google or Microsoft accounts. Reduces password fatigue and leverages existing enterprise identity infrastructure.

Volume Licensing (Enterprise)

Flexible multi-seat licensing for teams up to 50 devices with centralized license management and invoice-based billing.

Device Binding

Desktop licenses are bound to specific devices using secure device fingerprinting. Prevents unauthorized license sharing.

4.2 Session Management

  • JWT-based session tokens with configurable expiration (default: 1 hour)
  • Refresh tokens with 30-day lifetime
  • Automatic session invalidation on password change
  • Concurrent session limits per subscription tier
  • Remote session termination capability

4.3 Role-Based Access Control

Role Permissions Use Case
User Create/edit own projects, export, manage own settings Standard team member
Team Lead User permissions + view team usage, share projects Department manager
Admin All permissions + manage users, view all projects, billing IT administrator
Owner Admin permissions + manage admins, delete organization Account owner

5. Infrastructure Security

5.1 Cloud Infrastructure

TermiVoxed cloud services are hosted on Google Cloud Platform and Firebase, which provide:

  • SOC 1, SOC 2, SOC 3 compliance
  • ISO 27001, ISO 27017, ISO 27018 certifications
  • Physical security with 24/7 monitoring
  • Redundant power, cooling, and network connectivity
  • Regular third-party security audits

5.2 Network Security

  • Firewall: Cloud-native firewall rules with default-deny policy
  • DDoS Protection: Google Cloud Armor for L3/L4/L7 DDoS mitigation
  • CDN: Content delivery through Firebase Hosting CDN
  • VPN: Available for Enterprise customers requiring private connectivity

5.3 Monitoring & Logging

  • Centralized logging with Cloud Logging
  • Real-time alerting for security events
  • 90-day log retention (extended for Enterprise)
  • Audit logs for administrative actions
  • Anomaly detection for unusual access patterns

10. Security FAQ

Q: Where is my video content stored?

Desktop App: Videos are processed locally on your machine and never uploaded to our servers. Output files are saved to your specified local directory.

Web App: Videos are temporarily uploaded for processing and deleted within 24 hours after export completion. You can also choose to save projects to cloud storage for future editing.

Q: Who can access my voice cloning samples?

Voice samples are encrypted and accessible only by your account. They are used solely for generating voice clones within your projects. We do not use voice samples for training our models or any other purpose. Samples can be deleted at any time from your account settings.

Q: Do you use my content to train AI models?

No. We do not use customer content (videos, audio, voice samples, or scripts) to train our AI models. Your content is used solely for providing the service you requested.

Q: How do you handle third-party AI services (like OpenAI)?

If you use AI script generation with third-party providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.), your text prompts are sent to those providers under their terms of service. You can: (1) Use local AI models via Ollama with no external transmission, or (2) Provide your own API keys, giving you control over the data relationship.

Q: Can you provide a penetration test report?

We conduct annual third-party penetration testing. A summary report is available to Enterprise customers under NDA. Contact info@lxusbrain.com to request access.

Q: Do you have a bug bounty program?

Yes. We welcome responsible security research. Please report vulnerabilities to security@lxusbrain.com. We commit to acknowledging reports within 48 hours and providing updates on remediation progress.

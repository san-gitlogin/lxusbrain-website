Frequently Asked Questions

Can we start with a pilot before full deployment?

Yes. We offer a 30-day paid pilot at 50% of the standard pricing with up to 10 users. If you proceed with full deployment, pilot costs are credited toward your first invoice.

What happens if we need to add more users mid-contract?

Additional users can be added at any time at the applicable tier rate. The license will be pro-rated for the remaining contract period.

Is there a minimum user count for Enterprise?

Enterprise is designed for teams of 10+ users. For smaller teams, we recommend our Pro plan which offers most features at a lower per-user cost.

What data security measures are in place?

TermiVoxed uses AES-256 encryption for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit, and is hosted on secure cloud infrastructure. We are GDPR and India DPDP Act compliant. A detailed security whitepaper is available upon request.

Can we get a custom SLA?

Yes. Standard Enterprise includes 99.5% uptime SLA. We offer 99.9% SLA as an add-on. Custom SLA terms can be negotiated for contracts over Rs. 50 lakhs annually.

Do you offer on-premise deployment?

TermiVoxed is primarily a cloud-based service, but our desktop application can run entirely offline with local processing. For air-gapped environments, please contact us to discuss hybrid deployment options.

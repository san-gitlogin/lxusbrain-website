Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated on Dec 26, 2025

LxusBrain believes in helping its customers as far as possible, and has therefore a liberal cancellation policy. Under this policy:

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellations will be considered only if the request is made within 7 days of placing the order/subscribing to the service.
  • For subscription services, you may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
  • Once a subscription is cancelled, it will not auto-renew, but no refund will be provided for the remaining period unless requested within the 7-day window.

Refund Policy

  • Refund requests must be made within 7 days of purchase.
  • For digital services, refunds are processed if the service did not meet the described functionality or if you experienced significant technical issues that we could not resolve.
  • If you feel that the service received is not as described on the site or as per your expectations, you must bring it to the notice of our customer service within 7 days of purchasing. The Customer Service Team after looking into your complaint will take an appropriate decision.
  • In case of any Refunds approved by LxusBrain, it will take 3-5 business days for the refund to be processed to the end customer.

Non-Refundable Cases

  • Requests made after 7 days from purchase date
  • If you have extensively used the service (more than 5 exports)
  • Subscription renewals (you can cancel to prevent future charges)
  • Accounts terminated due to Terms of Service violation
